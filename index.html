Introduction

Started by a group of enthusiastic IETians, in 2001, in order to provide a grooming stage to the budding talents in the Institute, Aakshank has transformed itself into the most zestful and extraordinary cultural bonanza in Central India.
    This megafest include wide array of events ranging from competitions in varied geners like Dance, Drama, Singing to Star Night and DJ Night, Stunt Mania and plenty of informals.
